About This Tool

This converter turns JPG and PNG images into a single PDF — one image per page. Every image you add appears as a live thumbnail in page order, so you can see exactly how the finished PDF will read; rearrange with the arrows and the numbering updates instantly. Like every tool on this site, it runs entirely in your browser: your photos are never uploaded anywhere, which matters when they are photos of ID cards, certificates, or signed pages.

A4 or Original Size?

  • A4 (recommended for documents): each image is centered on a standard A4 page with a small margin, using landscape orientation automatically for wide images. This is what portals, printers, and offices expect.
  • Same size as image: the page exactly matches each image's pixel dimensions. Best for photos and graphics where you don't want white borders.

Better Results with Photos of Paper

If your images are phone photos of paper documents, you'll get a cleaner and far smaller PDF by using a scanning app instead of the plain camera — it straightens the page and removes shadows. See our full guide on scanning documents with your phone. If the resulting PDF is too large to email, see how to reduce PDF file size. You can also combine the output with existing PDFs using the merger.
