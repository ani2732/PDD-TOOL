About This Tool

This tool removes unwanted pages from a PDF — blank scanner pages, duplicated sheets, or sections that shouldn't be shared. Every page previews as a thumbnail; click the ones to remove and they fade with a delete mark, so you can see the result before committing. Processing happens entirely in your browser; the file is never uploaded.

Common Uses

  • Blank pages from double-sided scanning: automatic document feeders often insert empty pages — spot them instantly in the preview and click them away.
  • Removing sensitive pages before sharing: drop the salary annexure from a contract copy, or internal notes from a client file. (Note: this removes whole pages; it does not redact content within a page.)
  • Trimming oversized filings: combined with the compressor, deleting unneeded pages is the fastest way to get under a portal's upload limit.

Need to keep only a few pages rather than delete a few? The Split tool works the opposite way — click the pages you want to keep. And if pages are in the wrong order rather than unwanted, use Organize Pages.
